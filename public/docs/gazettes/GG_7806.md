## COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA

No. 215

NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Telecommunications Service Licences

- a) The Authority resolved to award Mainmast Electronics CC a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service License (ECNS and ECS).
- b) The Authority resolved to extend the period within which to provide telecommunications services for a period of twelve (12) months from 14 April 2022 to 13 April 2023, in respect of the Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service License (ECNS and ECS) awarded to Metagalaxy Space Science and Technology CC .

## 2. Broadcasting Service Licences

- a) The Authority resolved to renew the Community Broadcasting Service Licence awarded to Southern Sun Media Trust for a period of five (5) years.
- b) The Authority resolved to renew the Commercial Broadcasting Service Licence awarded to One Africa Television (Pty) Ltd for a period of ten (10) years.

The above decisions are effective from the date of publication of this Notice.

## General Notices

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of publication of this Notice.

**H. M. GAOMAB II**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
