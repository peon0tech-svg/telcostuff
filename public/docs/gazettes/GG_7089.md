The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of regulation 34(1) of the Regulations Prescribing Procedures Regarding Application for, and Amendment, Renewal, Transfer and Cancellation of Spectrum Licenses, published as General Notice No. 104, Government Gazette No. 6888, on 29 April 2019, hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Application for withdrawal of Spectrum Licence

- a) The Authority resolved to approve the withdrawal of the following Spectrum Licences awarded to Mwireless (Pty) Ltd , and recover the outstanding spectrum fees:

(29507.5 MHz (centre frequency) (Uplink) with bandwidth of 5 MHz; and 19707.5 MHz (centre frequency) (Downlink) with bandwidth of 5 MHz).

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, No.8 of 2009, the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this notice.
