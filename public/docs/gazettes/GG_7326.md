No. 362

## NOTICE OF RECONSIDERATION IN TERMS OF REGULATION 36(1) OF THE REGULATIONS PRESCRIBING PROCEDURES REGARDING APPLICATION FOR, AND AMENDMENT, RENEWAL, TRANSFER AND CANCELLATION OF SPECTRUM LICENSES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of regulation 36(1) of the Regulations Prescribing Procedures Regarding Application for, and Amendment, Renewal, Transfer and Cancellation of Spectrum Licenses, published as General Notice No. 104, Government Gazette No. 6888, on 29 April 2019, hereby revokes the General Notice No. 285 as published in Government Gazette No. 7273, dated 15 July 2020 and herewith gives notice of the following decision:

The Authority resolved to reconsider its decision as published in Government Gazette No. 7024, General Notice No. 425 dated 17 October 2019 and approve an application for amendment of the spectrum licence of Telecom Namibia Limited, to authorize the utilization of a contiguous block of 40 MHz spectrum (2308-2348MHz) for IMT services within the entire boundaries of the Republic of Namibia subject to the spectrum license conditions.

The full reasons for the decision can be requested from the Authority. As set out in section 32(2) of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), any person who has a substantial interest in this decision may take this decision on review within a period of six months from the date on which that person has become aware of this decision.

**H. M. GOAMAB II**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
