The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of Regulation 11(9) of the 'Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences and Spectrum Use Licences', in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), herewith gives notice that, pursuant to the provisions of section 35(2) of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No 8 of 2009) it has approved the transfer of interest of the Telecommunications Service Licence and all Spectrum Use Licences of Mobile Telephone Networks Business Solutions Namibia (Pty) Ltd as follows:

## DECISION OF THE AUTHORITY

Mobile Telephone Networks Business Solutions Namibia (Pty) Ltd (hereinafter referred to as 'MTN Business Nam'), was granted an exemption in terms of section 46(2) of the Communications Act, in respect of its foreign shareholding of 100% by MTN Group Limited by the Minister of ICT on 2 September 2013. The exemption was subject to the condition that 30% of its registered stock devolves to Namibian shareholders within three (3) years from 2 September 2013. This decision was published as General Notice No. 41 in Government Gazette No. 5411 dated 17 February 2014.

It must be noted that this is a notification in terms of section 35(2) as the transaction does not result in a transfer of control. In terms of regulation 7(2) of the 'Regulations Regarding licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences and Spectrum Use Licences' a Transfer of ownership interests not resulting in a transfer of control does not require prior approval from the Authority, however the Authority must be notified of the impending change.

However, owing to the fact that the transaction resulted from a license condition, the Authority is required to review it to ensure compliance with the license condition and make a new resolution in respect of the licence award.

The Authority thus approves the award of a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Services (ECS and ECNS) to Mobile Telephone Networks Business Solutions Namibia (Pty) Ltd, subject only to the provisions of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No 8 of 2009) and Regulations regarding Licence Conditions for Telecommunications Service Licences as published in Government Gazette No. 5037, Notice No. 308 dated 13 September 2012.

The approved transfer of interest will be as follows:

| New Owner                        | New Percentage of Shares   | Nationality   |
|----------------------------------|----------------------------|---------------|
| MTN Business Solutions (Pty) Ltd | 70%                        | South African |
| Profile Technologies (Pty) Ltd   | 30%                        | Namibian      |

Any party aggrieved by this decision may file an application for reconsideration in terms of section 31 of the Communications Act. Some of the information in this decision has been excluded from this notice due to the fact that it was submitted as confidential pursuant to the provisions of section 28 of the Communications.
