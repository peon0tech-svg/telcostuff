NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Applications in respect of Telecommunications Service Licences

- a) The Authority resolved to award a Class Telecommunications Service Licence (Non-Profit ECNS) to Wanderport Namibia Trust .
- b) The Authority resolved to award a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service Licence (ECS and ECNS) to Pektech Technologies CC.

## 2. Applications for in respect of Broadcasting Service Licences

- a) The Authority resolved to renew the Community Broadcasting Service Licence of Channel Seven Media for Christ .
- b) The Authority resolved to renew the Commercial Broadcasting Service Licence of Radio 100 (Pty) Ltd.

## General Notices

- c) The Authority resolved to renew the Community Broadcasting Service Licence of the University of Namibia t/a Unam Radio.
- d) The Authority resolved to renew the Commercial Broadcasting Service Licence of EFM Radio CC.
- e) The Authority has resolved to approve the application for the withdrawal of the Commercial Broadcasting Service License and Spectrum Licences awarded to Trustco Mixed Marketing (Pty) Ltd.

The decisions above are effective from date of this Notice.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this notice.

**H. M. GAOMAB II**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
