## SCHEDULE 1

## AMENDMENT OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING RULE-MAKING PROCEDURES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of sections 129 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), amends the Regulations Regarding Rule-Making Procedures published under General Notice No. 334 of 2010, as set out in the Schedule.

## Definition

1. In these regulations 'the Regulations' means the Regulations Regarding RuleMaking Procedures published under General Notice No. 334 of 2010.

## 2. Amendment of regulation 4 of Regulations

4(2) The Authority may initiate rule-making proceedings to make regulations prescribing anything that is required to be prescribed by this Act and prescribing anything that [are] is necessary or expedient to prescribe in order to implement the provisions of the Act. [even though the Act does not explicitly require such regulations to be prescribed.]

## Substitution of regulation 12 of Regulations

3. The Regulations is amended by the substitution for regulation 12 of the following regulation.

## 12. Reconsideration

- '(1) The Authority may, in terms of Section 31 of the Act reconsider any regulations made in terms of these Regulations, within 90 calendar days from the date of publication of the said regulations in the Gazette .
- (2) Any person (hereinafter 'the requesting party') may submit a request in writing to the Authority to reconsider any regulations made in terms of the rule-making procedures, within 30 days of the publication of the final regulations.
- (a) The requesting party must comprehensively set out its reasons for the request for reconsideration;
- (b) The Authority must within 14 days of receipt of the request for reconsideration determine that there are grounds for reconsideration;
- (c) Where there are no valid grounds for reconsideration, the Authority must forthwith notify the requesting party of its decision and consider the matter closed.
