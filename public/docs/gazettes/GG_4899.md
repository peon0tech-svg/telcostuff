The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia in terms of section 53(5) of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), herewith gives notice that every contract concluded for the provision of services that are not subject to a tariff must be filed with the Authority, which has the power to deal with such contract as if it were an interconnection agreement.

The Authority herewith gives notice to all telecommunications licensees, with an individual or prescribed class license, to file the abovementioned contracts. The contracts must:

- Contain all pertinent information concerning rates and charges for services, including all deposits, non-recurring charges and monthly charges;

- Contain terms and conditions applicable to the provision of services by the corresponding licensee including rights and remedies available to customers in the event of unauthorised charges or other disputes or claims over billing.

All contracts submitted must include a cover letter containing the name and contact details of the person submitting the contract and the name and contact details of the person for whom the contract is submitted, if different. The said contracts should be submitted within 30 days from date of publication of this notice.

All submissions must be made either physically or electronically -

- (1) By hand to the head offices of the Authority, namely Communications House, 56 Robert Mugabe Avenue, Windhoek;
- (2) By post to the head offices of the Authority, namely Private Bag 13309, Windhoek 9000;
- (3) By electronic mail to the following address: cran@cran.na;
- (4) By facsimile to the following facsimile number: +264 61 222790; or
- (5) By fax to e-mail to: 088642748.
