No. 596

## NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 4(1) OF THE REGULATIONS PRESCRIBING PROCEDURES REGARDING APPLICATION FOR, AND AMENDMENT, RENEWAL, TRANSFER AND CANCELLATION OF SPECTRUM LICENSES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of regulation 4(1) of the Regulations prescribing Procedures regarding Application for, and Amendment, Renewal, Transfer and Cancellation of Spectrum Licenses, published as General Notice No. 104, Government Gazette No. 6888, on 29 April 2019, herewith gives notice that the following radio frequencies under the Frequency Band Plan are available for application to be conferred on a first-come-first-serve basis:

| Frequencies (MHz)     | Spectrum Blocks             | Geographical location        | Service to be provided   |
|-----------------------|-----------------------------|------------------------------|--------------------------|
| 3600 - 3800 MHz (TDD) | 3610 - 3650 MHz (1x 40 MHz) | Geographic (Private Network) | MOBILE (IMT)             |

Further, all applicants should note the following:

- (a) Applicants who intend to use the radio frequencies listed above must apply to the Authority for a Spectrum License.
- (b) Applicants must be in good standing with the Authority in that they should not have any outstanding fees.
- (c) To ensure equitable access to spectrum, applications are limited to Private networks in terms of sections 43(1) and (2)(a) of the Communications Act and regulation 5(1)(f) of the Spectrum Licensing Regulations.

Applications must be sent or delivered to -

- (a) by hand at CRAN, Freedom Plaza, Courtside Building, c/o Fidel Castro and Rev. Michael Scott Streets, Windhoek;
- (b) by post to CRAN, Private Bag 13309, Windhoek, Namibia, 9000; or
- (c) by electronic mail to licensing@cran.na.

**E. NGHIKEMBUA**  
CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
