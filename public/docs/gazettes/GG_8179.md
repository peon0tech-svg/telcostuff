## NOTICE IN TERMS OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES AND SPECTRUM USE LICENCES

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences and Spectrum Use Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), herewith gives notice that it has approved:

1. Transfer of Licence and Transfer of Control of Licence as submitted by 99 FM (Pty) Ltd, which came into force and effect on the date of publication , notwithstanding the date of publication of the notice in the Gazette ; and
2. Transfer of Licence and Transfer of Control of Licence as submitted by One Africa Television (Pty) Ltd, which came into force and effect on the date of publication , notwithstanding the date of publication of the notice in the Gazette ; and
3. Transfer of Licence and Transfer of Control of Licence as submitted by Converged Telecommunication Solutions (Pty) Ltd, which came into force and effect on the date of publication in the Gazette .

Please note that the full submission and reasons for approval can be obtained from the Authority.
