No. 317

NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Applications

- a) The Authority resolved to decline Urban Café Radio CC 's application for a Commercial Broadcasting Service Licence.
- b) The Authority resolved to award Karubeams Crime Prevention Forum a Class Non-profit ECNS Telecommunications Service License and decline their application for a Spectrum Licence.
- c) The Authority resolved to award the Lizalex Communication Services (Pty) Ltd a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service License (ECNS and ECS).
- d) The Authority resolved to grant Atlas Tower Investment (Pty) Ltd an extension to commence utilisation of its Class Network Facilities telecommunications service licence for a further period of twelve (12) months, from the date of this Notice.
- e) The Authority resolved to grant Nexit Investment CC an extension to commence broadcasting services within twelve (12) months from the date of this Notice.
- f) Authority resolved that Mobile Telecommunications (Pty) Ltd must be listed on the Namibian Stock Exchange within a period of twenty four (24) months from 05 September 2020, of which 29% of the shares should be allocated as free float and sold to private local investors subject to sections 35 and 46 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009).

## 2. Lapse of Licenses

- a) The Authority noted that the Commercial Broadcasting Service Licence awarded to Namibia TV &amp; DVD Productions CC automatically lapsed on 21 December 2019.
- b) The Authority noted that the Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service Licence (ECS and ECNS) awarded to Excellent Communication (Pty) Ltd automatically lapsed on 03 December 2019.
- c) The Authority noted that the Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service Licence (ECS and ECNS) awarded to Loc8 Mobile CC automatically lapsed on 21 December 2019.
- d) The Authority noted that the Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service Licence (ECS and ECNS) awarded to Mantle Investment (Pty) Ltd automatically lapsed on 29 March 2019

The above decisions are effective from date of this Notice.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No.8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this notice.

**H. M. GAOMAB II**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
