## THE FOLLOWING ARE THE REASONS FOR THE DECISION:

Demshi Investment Holdings (Pty) Ltd (hereinafter referred to as 'the Applicant') was awarded a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service Licence (ECS &amp; ECNS) to provide telecommunications services within the borders of the Republic of Namibia on 9 September 2015. The licence was awarded in terms of the Regulations regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences and Spectrum Use Licences as published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272 dated 29 August 2015.

The Applicant intends to provide voice and data services and requested condonation to commence commercial services based on the fact-

- (i) That the Authority is still to make a decision in respect of a number range to allow the Applicant to provide telecommunications services; and
- Iii) That Telecom Namibia Limited is not responding to the Applicant's requests for infrastructure sharing.

Based on the inability of the Authority to consider the request for a number range submitted by the Applicant due to the pending Numbering Regulations and that it is a prohibiting factor to the launch of telecommunications services by the Applicant in that the Applicant is unable to provide voice services without a number range assigned, the Authority granted condonation to the Applicant to commence services until 9 September 2016.

The Numbering Regulations were approved by the Authority on 19 February 2016 and the final notice was published in the Government Gazette on 1 April 2016.

The Applicant submitted an application for a block of 100,000 numbers in the leading digit '82' number range on 28 June 2016 to provide services as provided for under its telecommunications service licence.

## PROCEDURAL COMPLIANCE

Following due process in terms of the Regulations prescribing the National Numbering Plan for Use in the Provision of Telecommunications Services in the Republic of Namibia, Numbering Licence Fees and Procedures for Number Procedures, the Authority published a notice in the Government Gazette 6074, General Notice No. 259, dated 18 July 2016, allowing fourteen (14) days for public comments from the date of publication of the Notice in the Gazette . The commenting period lapsed on 1 August 2016, and no comments were received.

## DECISION

In terms of section 81 of the Communications Act, the Authority herewith approves -

1. The award of a number licence to Demshi Investment Holdings (Pty) Ltd for a block of 100,000 numbers in the leading digit '82' number range for the provision of electronic communication services as set out in regulation 7 of the aforementioned Regulations.

Kindly take note that section 31 of the Communications Act provides that the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of making that decision or issuing that order.
