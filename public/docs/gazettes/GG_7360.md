No. 422

## NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Telecommunications Service Licence

- a) The Authority resolved to award Artemis Telecommunication (Pty) Ltd a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service License (ECNS and ECS).

## 2. Broadcasting Service Licence

- a) The Authority noted that the Broadcasting Service License (Signal Distribution) awarded to Paratus Telecommunications (Pty) Ltd automatically lapsed.
- b) The Authority resolved to approve the renewal of the Community Broadcasting Service Licence awarded to Shipi FM for a further period of five (5) years.

The decisions above are effective from date of this Notice.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this notice.

**H. M. GAOMAB II**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
