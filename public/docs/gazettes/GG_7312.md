No. 340

NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS PRESCRIBING LICENSE CATEGORIES AND LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR POSTAL SERVICE LICENSEES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Prescribing License Categories and Licensing Procedures for Postal Service Licensees, published in Government Gazette No. 7072, General Notice No. 506, dated 2 December 2019, hereby gives notice of the following decision:

## 1. Application for a Designated Postal Operator License

- a) The Authority resolved to award a Designated Postal Operator License to Namibia Post Limited , subject to the provisions of the Communications Act 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Postal License, and Regulations Prescribing License Categories and Licensing Procedures for Postal Service Licensees as published in Government Gazette No. 7072, General Notice No. 506, dated 2 December 2019.

The decision is effective from date of this Notice.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this Notice.

**H. M. GAOMAB II**

No. 343

NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS REGARDING LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND BROADCASTING SERVICE LICENCES: COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms Regulation 19(1) of the Regulations Regarding Licensing Procedures for Telecommunications and Broadcasting Service Licences, published in Government Gazette No. 4785, General Notice No. 272, dated 29 August 2011 (as amended), hereby gives notice of the following decisions:

## 1. Application for Renewal of Broadcasting Service License

- a) The Authority resolved to renew the Community Broadcasting Service Licence awarded to Khorixas Youth and Community Media Centre , for a further period of five (5) years, subject to a payment plan of outstanding fees.

## 2. Application for Reconsideration

- a) With effect from 24 July 2020, the Authority resolved to uphold its decision to award a Class Comprehensive Telecommunications Service License (ECNS and ECS) to the Municipal Council of the Municipality of Windhoek , which was published as General Notice No. 155, in Government Gazette No. 7196, on 29 April 2020. The decision is subject to the provisions of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), and additional license conditions. Further, the Municipal Council of the Municipality of Windhoek is required to submit quarterly compliance reports to the Authority in respect of the license conditions.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority.

In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), and with respect to decision 1. Above, the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of this Notice.

In terms of section 32 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), decision 2 may be taken on review by aggrieved party.
