## NOTICE IN TERMS OF SECTION 31 THE COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), hereby gives notice that Salt Essential IT (Pty) Ltd have submitted an application for reconsideration of the Authority's decision which was published as General Notice No. 556, in Government Gazette No. 7659 on 14 October 2021.

The full application for reconsideration can be requested from the Authority, and:

- a) The public may submit written comments within fourteen (14) days from the date of publication of this notice in the Gazette .
- b) The Applicant may submit a response to any written comments within fourteen (14) days from the lapsing of the time to submit written submissions.
- c) Written submissions must contain the name and contact details of the person making the written submissions and the name and contact details of the person for whom the written submissions is made, if different, and must be clear and concise.
- d) Written submissions and reply comments must be made by:
- (1) hand to Communications House, 56 Robert Mugabe Avenue, Windhoek;
- (2) post to Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, Private Bag 13309, Windhoek;
- (3) electronic mail to legal@cran.na;
- (4) facsimile to +264 61 222790; or
- (5) fax to e-mail to 0886550852.
