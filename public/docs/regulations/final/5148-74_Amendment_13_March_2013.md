## 1. Clarification on the PricingStructure andProductDescriptions

## 1.1 Pricing Structure

TelecomNamibiahasdefinednewTDMtransportserviceswithcleardifferentiationofhowthe different services are produced through our different network layers.

The new pricing model has also taken into account all thevarious network elements and activities

involvedinproducingthedifferentservicesin order todetermine thefinal pricepoints. WiththeoldLeasedlineservices,tariffsweredeterminedforeachelementofagivenserviceand added together toform a customer offer based on a given customer requirement or solution.Distance charges were calculated over the distance units presenting different price points for every incremental distanceunit.

Withthenewpricingstructure,asingletariffisdeterminedforatotalnetworksolutionwith all relevant elementsincluded in thepricepoint.Individualdistance chargesarenot applicablebut the pricing model defines a tariff applicable over a certain distance bracket per link speed.
