No. 694

## NOTICE IN TERMS OF REGULATION 19(1) OF THE REGULATIONS PRESCRIBING LICENCE CATEGORIES AND LICENSING PROCEDURES FOR POSTAL SERVICE LICENSEES. COMMUNICATIONS ACT, 2009

The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of regulation 19(1) of Regulations prescribing Licence Categories and Licensing Procedures for Postal Service Licensees, published as General Notice No. 506, in Government Gazette No. 7072, dated 2 December 2019, in accordance with the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), herewith gives notice that the Authority resolved to award the extension of the reserved services for Namibia Post Limited for an additional period of five (5) years as contemplated in the Licence Conditions awarded to Namibia Post Limited.

The full reasons for the decisions can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days of this notice.

**T. K. MUFETI**  
CHAIRPERSON OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS  
COMMUNICATIONS REGULATORY AUTHORITY OF NAMIBIA
